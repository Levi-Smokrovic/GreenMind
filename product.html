The hidden cost of cloud AI

Every time you ask ChatGPT, Gemini, or Copilot a question, massive data centers spin up GPUs that consume enormous amounts of electricity.

⚡

Extreme energy use

Training GPT-4 consumed an estimated 50 GWh — enough to power 18,000 homes for a year. Every query adds more. AI data centers are projected to consume more electricity than many countries by 2027.

💧

Massive water consumption

Data centers use millions of liters of water daily for cooling. A single GPT-4 conversation of 20–50 questions uses about 500ml of fresh water — like drinking a bottle for every chat session.

🔒

Your data, their servers

Cloud AI sends every message you type to remote servers. Your conversations, your questions, your private thoughts — stored, logged, and potentially used for training. You have zero control.
